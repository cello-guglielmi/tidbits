About

This site was built as a personal project by Marcello Guglielmi to share and organize inspiring quotes.

How To

  • Browse quotes/authors: The top Navbar contains links to the entire catalogue of quotes & authors currently in the database.
  • Submit a quote: The side Navbar has a Submit link that directs you to a page where you can fill out a quote submission form. Your submission is then forwarded to the moderation team for approval.
  • View my submissions: Your current pending submissions (as well as submission history) are displayed under your Profile. You can check their information & status there.
  • Once approved, a list of your contributions to the side can be viewed under Entries! Thank you for helping us grow!
  • Bookmark a quote: You can save your favorite quotes on the site via the BOOKMARK button. Bookmarked quotes can be accessed later in the Marked section.

FAQ

  • "Why was my submission rejected?" — Approval of submissions can depend on a variety of reasons. A Moderator will typically include a comment upon approval or rejection of a submission, that can be viewed under the specific submission card on your Profile. Additionally, you likely received an email with said comment.
  • "What's taking so long to review my submission?" — The reviewing process can be lengthy at times, specially if the quote was attributed to an author not currently present in the databse. We appreciate your understanding and patience!
  • "Do you accept quotes in other languages?" — Currently, the site's implementation is limited to English. However, official/accurate translations are OK on a case by case basis.
